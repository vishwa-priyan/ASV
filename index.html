About us

Our company voluntarily provides all the seeds and fertilizers to the farmers and they cultivate their fields using our fertilizers and seeds and we purchase agricultural commodities from them. We attach great importance to this and cultivate them in large quantities and export them to foreign countries.

Our Vision


To be a global leader in sustainable agricultural exports, providing high-quality products that enrich lives and contribute to the growth of communities worldwide.

Our Mission


Our mission is to connect farmers to global markets by exporting top-quality agricultural products, ensuring fair trade, promoting sustainability, and creating lasting value for our customers, employees, and stakeholders.

Our Exports


At ASV Agri Export, we take immense pride in delivering the finest agricultural products to markets worldwide.

Our Objective


  • To expand our product range and export to at least 10 new countries by the next 3 years.
  • To maintain the highest standards of quality control, ensuring that every product meets or exceeds international agricultural export regulations.
  • To develop strong relationships with local farmers, ensuring fair pricing and sustainable farming practices that benefit both producers and consumers.
  • To implement innovative technologies and practices that minimize the environmental impact of our operations while increasing efficiency and productivity.
  • To foster long-term partnerships with global distributors and retail
